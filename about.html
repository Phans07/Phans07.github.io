About Us

Welcome to Phan Apparel, your number one source for all things fashion. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of our clothing line, with an emphasis on quality, comfort, and value.

Founded in 2019 by Steven Phan, Phan Apparel has come a long way from its beginnings in London, ON. When Steven Phan first started out, his passion for fashion drove them to start their own business.

Feel free to experience our favourite collection of shirts, pants, shoes, and more! We hope you enjoy the products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
